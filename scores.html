Personality Scores Analysis

In this section we aim to analyze Agreeableness and Openness scores of alcohol users who consumed alcohol previous day based on their responses in the research. The Agreeableness score shows that women tend to agree more than men, indicating women has higher Agreeableness score. On the other hand, we can the Openness score show a higher score for men compare to women. Indicating men to have a higher Openness score after consuming alcohol. The correlation matrix shows the correlation between personality scores. .
